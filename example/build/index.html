Specifying ECMAScript.

Who?

The specification is standardized by the TC39 (Technical committee 39) of ECMA (European Computer Manufacturers Association). You can read ourthe scope of our work here

How?

We have afour stage processwith a rolling release schedule with a new specification published every year.

Proposals move through stages at TC39 meetings, which are held everytwo months, and theNotesare made public.

Want to get involved?

There are many opportunities to get involved on an individual level. Check out ourContributor Documentationand our guides to learn more. To get involved with the committee on an organizational level, read ourJoining the TC39documentation.
